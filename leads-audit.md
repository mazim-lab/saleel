## Summary Findings

The audit reveals a significant inaccuracy rate regarding the "Website Status" column. A majority of the businesses marked as having "None" or "Directory listing only" actually have functional, dedicated websites.

*   **Total Checked:** 11
*   **Passed (Confirmed No Website):** 5 (45%)
*   **Failed (Actually Has Website):** 6 (55%)
*   **Overall Accuracy:** Low (~45%) regarding website status.
*   **Existence Check:** 100% (All businesses checked appear to exist and are active).

## Recommendations

1.  **Re-verify the entire list:** The "Website Status" column is unreliable. Over 50% of the "no website" leads actually have websites.
2.  **Filter Strategy:** Before sending cold emails pitching web design services, you must verify the remaining leads manually or programmatically. Pitching a website to a business that already has a modern one (`spotlessautomotive.ca` is quite modern) will damage credibility.
3.  **Target Adjustment:** The leads that *failed* the audit (have websites) might still be prospects for **SEO or Redesign** services, but they are not "no website" leads.
